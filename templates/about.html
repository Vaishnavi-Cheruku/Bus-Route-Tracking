Routes Page

Welcome to the college bus route tracking system. This platform is designed to help students, faculty, and staff easily find and track the college bus routes. Below are some of the features and information you can find on this page:

  • Detailed route maps
  • Estimated arrival times
  • Notifications for delays and schedule changes
  • Bus stop locations

Our goal is to ensure a smooth and efficient transportation experience for everyone at the college. Please use the navigation bar to explore the different routes and their schedules.

How to Use the Tracking System

To track your bus in real-time, follow these simple steps:

  1. Select your route from the navigation menu.
  2. View the live map to see the current location of your bus.
  3. Check the estimated arrival time at your stop.
  4. Set up notifications to stay informed about any changes or delays.
